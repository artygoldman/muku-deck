Every section of this brand was built the same way: real data in → analysis with stated assumptions → a concrete output. Below, per section, is what we investigated, the sources we used, and how each conclusion was derived.

1 · Market & Trends

Question: is there real demand for a premium bamboo bathtub tray in Japan — and how big / how stable?

Investigated

Cross-channel demand signals

  • Search-result volume per channel
  • Head terms & related keywords
  • Seasonality / spikes vs. steady
Amazon.co.jpRakutenYouTubeTwitter/XSerpApi · Apify · scrape
→
Method

Trends matrix

  • Pull live counts per channel
  • Normalize & compare signals
  • Classify market size & stability
  • Flag head terms / content gaps
→
Result

Real but niche demand

  • Small, steady market — not a viral spike
  • Clear head terms to own
  • Unverified/sparse signals labelled as hypotheses

Premises: search-result counts used as a demand proxy; channels weighted by buyer relevance; where data is thin it is marked “hypothesis / unverified”, not stated as fact.
